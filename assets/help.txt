OPTION          USAGE               DEFAULT EXPLANATION                                             
--help          --help                      Displays this list.
--fast          --fast              false   Updates the entire render at once, which is faster.
--offset        --offset {int}      1       Controls how many generations the program evaluates
                                            until termination, low is more generations.
--resolution    --resolution {int}  512x512 Controls the resolution of the render.
--zoom          --zoom {double}     0.0     Controls the initial zoom of the render.
--real          --real {double}     0.0     Controls the initial real position of the render.
--imag          --image {double}    0.0     Controls the initial imaginary position of the render.
