CONTROL         ACTION                                                                              
Esc             Close the render.
H               Displays this text.
ArrowUp         Increase the offset of the render. Also refreshes the image. Hold down Ctrl to
                change it more.
ArrowDown       Decrease the offset of the render. Also refreshes the image. Hold down Ctrl to
                change it more.
LeftMouse       Zoom in, centered on the point where you click.
C               Toggle color mode
